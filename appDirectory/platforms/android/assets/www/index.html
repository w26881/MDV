Week 1

  1. Twitter could be used to authenticate to third party services. Use the service you already have a relationship with (Twitter) to authenticate to new services. It could also be used to authenticate payments. It is very convenient especially since I am sure we are all tired of filling in web forms and creating accounts for every site on the net.

  2. We need JSONP to facilitate cross-domain communication and resource sharing.

  3. Geolocation pairs well with many things from providing metadata for created media like photos to checking in at a location or allowing a service to know if you are in the vicinity of an affiliated physical establishment. Notifications allow third party services to push important updates and offerings to the device. Contacts allows easy access to your relationships and can leverage that access to provide actionable features.

  4. I hope to integrate maps, geolocation, camera, and contacts into my app for tracking relationships. It is like having a dossier on each of the people in your life.

  5. Some past 'lessons learned' regarding workflow as it relates to research, design, development, etc. are to keep it simple and taking every opportunity to be immersing myself in the subject matter and searching the internet for inspiration. I have very little time due to the circumstances of my life so I have to be vigilant in not wasting any of it. So any free moment I get, I am going through lessons, or troubleshooting or debugging, or just setting up a framework for when I do get some time.
